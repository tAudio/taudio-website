We are tAudio.

An Israel-established startup aiming to develop advanced DSP algorithms and exciting new musical instruments.
With the power of new technologies and our vast experience in pro audio, we have the opportunity to fabricate our dreams and ideas with the goal of giving others new ways of creating their art.
